FEATURES

What it does

01

12-Voice Step Sequencer

Twelve independent drum voices on a 16-step grid. Each voice has its own lane — lay down kicks, snares, hats, claps, and percussion without fighting for space.

02

Per-Step Probability & Velocity

Set the probability and velocity of every individual step. Add ghost notes, random fills, or stuttering hits that keep a loop from feeling like it's on rails.

03

Per-Channel Sound Shaping

Level, tune, decay, tone, and drive per channel. Dial in each hit without reaching for an EQ — punch up the kick, tighten the snare, roll off the hat.

04

Eight Pattern Slots

Store up to eight separate patterns and switch between them live. Build verse, chorus, breakdown, and fill variations and chain them into a full arrangement.

05

Swing & BPM Control

Global swing control adds feel to rigid step patterns. Pair with tempo sync to lock to your DAW, or run the internal clock for standalone use.

06

Built for Speed

Everything on one screen. Click a step to toggle it, drag to set velocity, right-click to set probability. Fast enough to keep up with an idea before it disappears.
