FEATURES

What it does

01

Smart Voice Leading

As you move between chords, Keydence finds the closest voicing — minimising the distance each note travels. Smooth changes, no jarring jumps.

02

Real-Time Inversion Control

Dial through chord inversions live while you play. Each step reshapes the voicing, cascading through the same harmony from a different angle.

03

Key Mode

Lock to a key and every note you hit automatically generates a chord that fits. No wrong notes, no theory required.

04

Chord Type Control

Switch between chord types and stack modifiers on the fly — maj, min, dom7, add9, sus, and more.

05

Works With Any Sound

Pure MIDI out — plug it in front of any instrument in your DAW. Your synth, your piano, your sampler. Keydence just handles the harmony.

06

Zero Latency

MIDI only — no audio processing overhead. Completely transparent in any DAW signal chain.
