FEATURES

What it does

01

16-Step Grid Engine

Paint kick, snare, hat, bass, and lead patterns on a 16-step grid. Chain sections together for full arrangements, or loop a single idea indefinitely.

02

Per-Step Control

Every step has its own probability, velocity, ratchet, accent, glide, and note value. Individual hits change character without breaking the flow.

03

True Sound Locks

Lock a specific sound to any step in a pattern. Swap kits underneath and the locked hit stays put — ideal for accents and signature moments that define a loop.

04

Built-In Kits & Presets

Ship-ready kits and pattern presets for fast starting points. Kit morphing blends between two kits across steps — evolving textures without manual resampling.

05

MIDI & WAV Export

Export patterns as MIDI for further editing, or bounce stems as WAV. MIDI Learn on every control means your hardware can take over at any time.

06

Audio-Reactive Visuals

The UI reacts to its own output — hits pulse, levels animate, patterns breathe. It feels like a compact idea machine, not a spreadsheet.
